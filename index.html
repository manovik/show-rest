About Our Restaurant
Established in November of 2019, Luxury Restaurant has quickly achieved respect and success within the region, rising to one of the top ten restaurants on OpenTable among all metropolitan Baton Rouge restaurants. Our Chef and his team have found an ideal balance between Cajun culture, European tradition, and cutting edge flavors that the public can’t get enough of. Nothing pleases us more than to hear over and over again, “That was the best meal I’ve ever had”.
